Sleep different for everyone!

On average, most dogs spend about 50% of their day sleeping—about 12 hours in a 24-hour period.
More than half of cats sleep between 12 and 18 hours a day and nearly 40% of cats sleep more than 18 hours per day
Experts recommend that adults sleep between 7 and 9 hours a night. Adults who sleep less than 7 hours a night may have more health issues than those who sleep 7 or more hours a night.
Newborns should get 14–17 hours of sleep over a 24-hour period, says the National Sleep Foundation.
